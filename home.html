Discover the Problem

What is DDT?

DDT is a chemical pesticide that, despite its effectiveness in controlling insects, has long-term harmful effects on ecosystems and human health. Its use has resulted in contamination of aquatic environments, threatening marine life and food chains.

Why Should You Care?

  • DDT bioaccumulates in the food chain, harming top predators and humans.
  • Contaminated water affects aquatic life and fishing industries.
  • DDT's toxicity poses risks to reproductive health and development.
