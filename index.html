What is DDT?

DDT (Dichlorodiphenyltrichloroethane) is an effective insecticide which is a harmful substance almost colorless, tasteless and odorless, highly toxic to humans and animals. Now, The barrels are falling apart contaminating the environment with DDT and other harmful chemicals. Researchers from Scripps institute of Oceanography are researching the contamination level and ways to stop them.
